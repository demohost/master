About Us

Master's Software Group has been providing e-Governance ERP system to Indian educational institutes/campuses over last 20+ years. We are India's most trusted ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) partner offering integrated solutions along with product portfolio built on the best practices followed by various institutes from across the country within the very Indian Educational Framework. These offerings can easily be tailored according to the specific institute's standards and processes thereby extending help to overcome the IT operational challenges of educational institutions. We have distinct product offering for the respective segments of education systems right from school management to college management, post graduation and universities, as well as for deemed or open Universities. We have already been trusted by more than 2000+ institutions including 50 MHRD governed and funded Institutions such as NITs, IIIT, central universities and major autonomous institutions. Our commitment to Education Sector for the last two decades has been to provide an advance educational system with innovative technology programs that prepare students for the workplace and for life.

Our Team:

Our team consists of 200+ technically qualified and highly skilled professionals who are expert in developing ERP solutions. The team is guided by a mentor panel comprising of Academicians, Global IT Experts, Technology Architects and Process Consultants. We routinely conduct workshops and trainings for keeping pace with the latest advancements in technologies to implement the same in our offering and services.

Why MasterSoft?

We understand the DNA of Educational System in India, having worked through 2000+ implementations since the year 2000. Our primary goal & commitment is exclusively for the education sector. Repository of best practices in-built in our products are unparalleled. We are prime movers in bringing to the market, latest advances such as attendance systems based on ultra high frequency(UHF) and RFID technology. We have also launched a low cost Cloud enabled integrated ERP for educational societies having schools (State, CBSE, IB, lCSE), colleges, polytechnics, engineering and medical colleges etc. for effective and integrated management of schools and college administration. This Cloud also offers a low cost ERP for smaller institutes. We are continuously improving to keep pace with the changing IT needs such as security, scalability, reliability & availability to ensure the right performance of the system. Adopting the "Social & Mobile" phenomenon - thereby giving the student a feel of learning in this digital AGE! Proven and tested support system readily available for any kind of challenges through implementation and post deployment facilitating the institutes for the required 'change management' needed. Always available at service and deliver the promise. Optimal deployment of every human, digital and resources to generate faster and higher ROI.
