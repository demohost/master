Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is your data safety policy?

MasterSoft provides you 100% data ownership. Nobody has direct access to the data layer. For further protection of your data, MasterSoft asks you to sign a data protection agreement that ensures data handover at any time in future. Institute has a direct access to data which can be downloaded in excel at any point.

Solution is hosted on highly secure cloud servers, under the Meghraj Policy in Non seismic zones, and multiple auto-backups are conducted, ensuring data security. Security checks & auto-prompts are triggered at sensitive action points.

How can MasterSoft SSR Module help you during the NAAC accreditation process?

MasterSoft NAAC SSR Module is comprehensive software that helps Higher Educational Institutions (HEIs) to collect, manage, assess, and analyze year-on-year data covering – Student, Faculty, and Administration Lifecycles systematically in chronological order. Date-stamped legacy data & reports ensure the highest accuracy & help stakeholders to satisfy the parameters required for getting NAAC accreditation. In addition to this, the software gives the provision of 100 data entry forms for saving data-related to social events, seminars, alumni & generates nearly 300 ready to upload NAAC SSR reports in the required format of NAAC. Institutions can also calculate & check the QnM responses for the NAAC assessment cycle.

How to get higher ROI with education MIS for colleges, universities & schools?

Be it a college, university or a school, proper data management is an important aspect. With Management Information System (MIS), institutions can collect, manage, access, and analyze the entire institutional data with ease. They can generate various reports related to different departments and keep a tab on every activity. With streamlined in-house processes & accurate institutional reports in hand, educators can make smart decisions which will eventually increase institutional efficiency and give highest ROI.

How does Education ERP help in Timetable Management?

Education ERP integrated with timetable management module can help you generate a clash-managed timetable by providing you details such as – Total number of faculty with specialization of their subjects, availability of teachers, classrooms, total number of students in a branch-wise and section-wise. Thus, it can help faculty members generate & manage daily, weekly & monthly timetable without any hassle. Teachers can also keep track of their unit-wise curriculum, lectures which were planned for them and lectures which were actually executed.

What is the MasterSoft Choice Based Credit System (CBCS)?

MasterSoft CBCS is an e-governance ERP software designed & developed in compliance with the parameters set by the University Grants Commission (UGC) for encouraging students to follow their passion & learn new skills. The system is integrated with automated functionalities that enable inter-departmental learning by allowing students to freely choose elective & ability enhancement courses of their interests. Moreover, performance assessment is done on the basis of credits and “letter grades” instead of the traditional method thus, providing learning flexibility.
