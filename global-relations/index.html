Lead Management

Mastersoft Lead Management System is a completely automated, integrated and smart solution to track, manage and retain your leads in the best possible manner via multiple sources and channels. You can funnel your leads efficiently through the system which has advanced tools for lead management and retention.

The system helps you to recruit students in the most effective way and prevent leakage of leads. Easy and intuitive to use, the solution saves time, increases productivity and automates most of your lead management process. Various kinds of reports provide business intelligence and assist you in taking data driven decisions.

Student Information Management System (SIMS)

A central, comprehensive and coherent system to organize your student information and maintain complete visibility. Mastersoft Student Information Management System handles unlimited amount of data securely, efficiently and transparently. With role based access and best possible encryption all your data is kept 100% secured.

Complete computerization of every process going on inside your campus. The solution channelizes all your resources and puts them to the best possible use by reducing efforts, saving time and removing repetition of work, so that you can focus on timely attainment of your academic as well as administrative goals pertaining to admission, fees, attendance, exam, etc.

Faculty Management System

The Faculty Management System ensures that all your faculty related data such as faculty attendance, leave, payroll along with human resource, is stored and organized accurately. The highly integrable software allows you to integrate the system with other smart technologies to record faculty attendance, etc.

Maintain your faculty related operations with enhanced visibility and absolute control over every process. Multitude of reports based on accurate data provides an insight into all the areas which need extra attention and also the possible causes. Using the consistent data provided by the system you can find out what works best for you.

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

The education ERP is an end-to-end ERP solution which facilitates complete digitalization of your institutional activities. The single system provides an integrated platform which facilitates standardization of activities throughout your campus. Mastersoft ERP contains all the necessary tools made out of the latest technology stack to streamline your processes.

The solution provides optimum systematization of various administrative sections such as inventory management, finance, accounts, HRMS, etc. The fully automated system ensures that all your key information is stored efficiently, systematically and facilitates effortless interdepartmental communication.
