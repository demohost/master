Mizoram University Testimonial

This is to certify that Mizoram University procured several modules of Proprietary e-Governance ERP RF-CAMPUS© from the Developer company "It is The Master's Software Nagpur" now known as "MasterSoft ERP Solutions Pvt. Ltd." vide our order no. No. 10/1/2010/(CC)/Office Automation/115 dated 21st July 2015. Major Modules procured are:

  • Admission Management (Online)
  • Automation of University Examinations
  • Asset & Estate Management
  • Health Center Management
  • Purchase, Stores & Inventory
  • Library Management
  • Affiliated College Management
  • E-Learning
  • Human Resource Management
  • Employee & Students Self Portals
  • Finance & Accounting
  • Mobile Apps. for Faculty & Students

This integrated e-Governance ERP RF-CAMPUS© is installed; customization are done and training is given to Mizoram University Users. The ERP is being used by MZU since Academic session 2015-16 successfully for all the important activities such as College Affiliation, Online Admissions & Online Fees collection, Conduct of University Examination & Result processing are performed using our ERP. The result of two sessions is declared successfully and completed the Admissions of 2016-17 batch using the ERP.

Other modules like Hostel management, Establishment & Payroll, Library Management, Store Module, Vehicle Management, Estate Management, Document Management, File Movement, Complaint Management, Health Management, IQAC etc., Finance & Accounts, e-Learning, T & P Stores are installed & customized as per the requirements.

The complete Student & Result data and Library data was imported successfully in new ERP RF-CAMPUS. The ERP Solution provided to us is an End to End Comprehensive System. Modern day tools like Payment Gateway, SMS & Email integration... has simplified our day to day working & communication. Mizoram University users are satisfied with ERP RF-CAMPUS & service support.

All MIS reports are designed as per the requirements of MZU and can be generated instantly from the system. The solution in terms of speed, accuracy, integrity & security is excellent.

Company is providing best on-site support by dedicated onsite engineers who take care of requirement gathering, user training, on-desk support and upgrade & maintenance of the MIS/ERP. Regular visits by senior team members from head office for review and various critical processes like Result Processing help us in accurate and faster Implementation.

We are sure that this ERP will be very useful for other Educational Campuses in India. The services provided by MasterSoft are excellent.
